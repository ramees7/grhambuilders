ABOUT US

Studio Brazil Interiors is an established firm of interior architects and has being working alongside Brazil Associates Architects for a number of years. The practice is a dynamic and innovative design-led firm committed to producing Interiors of quality and maintaining the highest professional standards.

Studio Brazil Interiors has built a particular reputation for its involvement in the design and renovation of many exemplary private residences, providing a professional bespoke service to clients. Studio Brazil have built up contacts with selected talented joiners, soft furnishing companies, carpet and fabric suppliers and curtain makers. The practice also has considerable experience and expertise in hotel, restaurant, commercial fit out and conservation projects.
